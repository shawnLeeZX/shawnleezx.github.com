Playbook for Strategic Foresight and Innovation Reading Notes Series -- Chapter Two

Dec 7, 2013

This chapter is a should-read-twice chapter. Similar to previous chapter, this chapter tries to provides a overview for reader. However, it is more specific. It breaks the innovation process down and dicuss them separately.

Actually, this is the second time that I have read this chapter – not the should-read-twice meaning what I meant just now. At the first time, the reading was not mandatory, so I did not persist reading all the book. And for the second time, I completely forget what I learnt at the first time. This chapter is hard to comprehend without action.

Now, the reading notes begin.

Why a playbook

At the beginning, the author stresses the role of this book – playbook.

A playbook is a notebook containing the descriptions and diagrams of all the possible plays and strategies used by a team.

This book is about coaching you and your teammates. And it is hard, like everything else that is great.

Some definitions

Then the author makes clear some definitions to make sure readers understand two important basic concept of this playbook.

Strategic Foresight

Acknowledge the ambiguity of the future. Use methods to get a view the future and influence it.

Innovation

This is what everyone knows, finding, building and taking a new idea into the marketplace.

Radical Innovation

The difference between Innovation and Radical Innovation is Radical Innovation create huge ideas which have huge impact.

Break down the innovation process

Then beginning with the start of the innovation process, the author breaks it down and discuss:

  1. What type of growth do we seek, which determines what kinds of ideas we want to think of.
  2. For an idea, where do we get our inspiration?
  3. The roles people play when the innovation process starts.
  4. The influence the contexts of industries will bring to the innovation process.

Learning process

After the breakdown of the innovation process, the author provides guide about how to learn and use the method provided in this playbook.

Finish

As I have written, this is a should-read-twice chapter. Or more precise, this is learning instructions. When finishing reading this book and going to apply the methods, go back and check the advice offered by the author.

There are 10 valuable advices at the end of this chapter.
