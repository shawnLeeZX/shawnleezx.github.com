Greetings

I am thinking to make this blog more serious, thinking about how to re-organize the content and cleansing things.

Whatever the final result is, this blog is going to be much less personal than it previously is and the personal part will be moved to a private place.

The initial motivation when I was making this blog was basically learning how to setup a blog-like website, which was solely a technical reason. Then due to the fact that I needed a person website for applying for graduate study, I further improved and polished the site. At the same time, I was trying to find somewhere that could be used to centralize my thoughts, which helps me a lot sort thing out, think things deeply, and keep a track of life. Then this pattern went for a few years. During this period, I did not care or expect people to read it. But if I pried the motivation a little deeper, there was also an action to show off, which is an inevitable period of the young when they are growing up.

A lot of things happened since then, I also blogged a lot of them, hiding specific details but keeping the lessons learned. Now, I realize that the functionality of this blog could be fully realized without publishing it to the world. The one that oriented to the world should be in another form, more organized, clearer and more specialized. This is almost the full story.

Another part of the story is that though I did not want too much traffic here, there was indeed some traffic in this blog, but people just grabbed things they need and went away. This was once also my bad habits when reading blogs, which is a really bad habits of Chinese people. We do not have the sense of community and do not take the responsibility to maintain it. I have a sense the lessons I learned should be helpful for others, especially for people having the same background with me — the suffering Chinese people getting their way to knowledge, freedom and a real life. But I got more misunderstanding rather than gratitude. This really taught me a lesson about how people think, learn, interact and the nature of human race. So through all those years’ experience, I decided to only help the people who want to change from their own heart.

I guess this is all about it. It may take months or years to get the site alive again. So leave some feedback if you were once a reader of it. Be a nice citizen.

What ones really can own at last are their stories. Just try to live a good one.

Shawn, June 19, 2015
