This is Shuai Li, also Shawn.

Research Interests

I’m currently a Master of Philosophy student at Chinese University of Hong Kong(CUHK). My research areas are Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, with current focus on Deep Learning on Computer Vision. I deem vision problem as an entry point to understand perception problems of Artificial Intelligence, which may be the first step to understand intelligence.

Short Bio

Before that, I was a Research Assistant at the same place and working on the same problem. Prior to then, I was a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia working on distributed machine learning techniques for learning intermediate representation of large scale text corpus. I received a B.E in Computer Science from Special Class for Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China(USTC).

General Interests

Product Design

Besides intellectual and philosophical curiosity, the impact of Machine Learning as a transcendental technology may have on civilization is my major motivation to work in this area. As a result, I have a side interest in Product Design, Human Computer Interaction and Hacking. In 2014, collaborating with students of USTC and Stanford University(SU), we designed an arm-mounted haptic navigation wearable device in Design and Innovation Course(ME310) of SU and our product was ranked the best five out of sixty teams in the China-US Maker Contest.

Engineering

In addition, I deem engineering is an inextricable part of scientific innovation. If looking back in history, the interaction between philosophers and artisans were one key element to give European society the dynamics Renaissance and Industrial Revolution needed. Its modern spiritual incarnation in our time is called a hacker. Consequently, I spare considerably efforts on programming skills. I am an enthusiastic Linux, Emacs, Vim user since 2011 so that I could directly touch the engineering technology I use without being abstracted away by the package providers.

Hobbies

BTW, I run often. I read, mostly on non-fiction, especially philosophy, history, biography. I enjoy music, mostly on light music, ancient Chinese style music. I bike when having time. I keep photographic memory on Flickr.

Epilogue

I initially created this site to understand how web applications work in 2013. Ever since then, this site has become my notebook to keep draft notes on everything but mainly on Computer Science, Mathematics, Research and Life.
