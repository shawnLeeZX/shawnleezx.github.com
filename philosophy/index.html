This is a page that collects notes written over the years to conceptualize a philosophy of our time. The goal is to bring back Natural Philosophy that enabled the Age of Reason in 18th century, and the Neo-Confucianism in Sung Dynasty that made Shi Ren 士人(Intellectuals of China) a social-economically significant social identity (for the first time in history). The philosophy should composed with rationality with humane touch (in my opinion, the two are the best parts of the Western and Eastern philosophy).
